About

I’m Duofeng. I am an EE student who enjoys photographing landscapes, wildlife, and moments of travel. This website is a personal project documenting a journey to North-west China. My work focuses on natural environments, wildlife, and travel, with an interest in quiet scenes rather than dramatic events. Through a slow and observational approach, this project reflects a personal way of seeing that values stillness and distance.
