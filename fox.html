FOX

Wanderer of the snow world, the red fox of Burqin County moves across the vast winter landscape like a small flame against the endless white. In the silent cold of the northern wilderness, its presence brings a subtle warmth and vitality to the frozen earth. Their behaviour is both curious and endearing. When they find food — or occasionally accept safe food provided by humans — they rarely eat it immediately. Instead, they carry it away to a hidden place, carefully burying it beneath the snow where no one can see. Only when they have finished foraging do they return, tossing the treasure back out of the snow before enjoying their well-earned meal.
