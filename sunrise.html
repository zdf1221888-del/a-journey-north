SUNRISE

Winter sunrises in the north often feel especially powerful. On freezing mornings, the warmth captured by the eyes arrives before the body can truly feel it. There is something hopeful and almost imaginative about that first light, as if it reminds us what warmth feels like after a long absence. This album includes photographs of the coloured sun pillars observed near Hemu Resort — a rare atmospheric phenomenon that appears in extremely cold regions when sunlight reflects off ice crystals suspended in the air. The collection also features sunrises at Swan Lake and Sayram Lake, where snow-covered landscapes, frozen waters, and distant mountains frame the rising sun and amplify the quiet drama of the morning light.
