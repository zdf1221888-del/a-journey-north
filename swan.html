SWAN

Flocks of swans move across the winter landscape like dancers upon a silent stage, bringing a sense of grace and softness to the harsh stillness of the cold season. Their white feathers echo the snow-covered earth, blending effortlessly with the quiet winter scenery. In the early morning, we arrived at the reserve while the frost still lingered on the ground. A thin veil of mist hovered above the water, and the swans had already begun their day. Some calmly preened their feathers, arranging their immaculate plumage; others chased and played across the water, scattering small ripples across the surface. A few drifted quietly as they searched for food, gliding through the tranquil lake with slow and deliberate movements.
